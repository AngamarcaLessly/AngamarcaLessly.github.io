HOW TO MAKE A CAKE

Ingredients you will need are

  • flour
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • butter
  • water
  • salt
  • a leavening agent(baking soda,etc)

Steps

  1. Mix flour and sugar and let it sit

  2. Seperate the yolk from the egg whites and mix (you can put the yolk in if you want)

  3. Add sugar into the eggs slowly while mixing

  4. Add the flour and sugar you mixed into the eggs

  5. place the outcome on a tube pan or bunt cake pan

  6. put it in a pre heated oven at 325 and leave it there for 20 minutes

  7. Then it's done
